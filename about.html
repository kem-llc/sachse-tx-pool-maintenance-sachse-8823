Our story

Your Local Pool Care Experts

To provide Sachse homeowners with reliable, high-quality pool maintenance that makes pool ownership effortless and enjoyable.

Sachse Pool Maintenance was founded by a team of pool enthusiasts who saw a need for dependable, local pool care. We started small, serving a handful of neighbors, and grew through word-of-mouth. Our commitment to punctuality, transparency, and excellence has made us a trusted name in Sachse.

Today, we maintain over 150 pools weekly. We invest in ongoing training and use top-tier equipment to deliver consistent results. Every technician is background-checked and trained to treat your property with respect. We're proud to be part of the Sachse community.
